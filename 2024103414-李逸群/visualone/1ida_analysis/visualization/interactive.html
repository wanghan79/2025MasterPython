Protein-Ligand Interaction Analysis

PDB File: 1ida.pdb

Feature Descriptions

  • Shape Complementarity: Measures how well the ligand fits into the binding site.
  • Electrostatics: Measures electrostatic interactions between charged atoms.
  • Hydrophobicity: Measures hydrophobic interactions in the binding site.
  • Hydrogen Bonds: Identifies potential hydrogen bonds between protein and ligand.
  • π-π Interactions: Identifies potential π-π stacking interactions between aromatic rings.
